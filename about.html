Why You Can choose us?

Welcome to our charming little ice cream shop, where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee fills the air and the warm ambiance invites you to relax and unwind. Nestled in the heart of Sungai Petani, our ice cream shop offers a unique and intimate setting, away from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Step through our doors, and you'll be greeted by friendly smiles, attentive service, and a genuine passion for ice creams.


Our small café was born out of a lifelong dream and a passion for creating a warm and inviting space for people of all ages can gather, relax, and indulge in their favorite frozen treats. The idea was conceived by our founder, Nor Adlina a passionate food enthusiast with a deep love for ice cream. After years of planning and preparation, our ice cream parlour finally opened its doors on July 2022. Situated in a quaint corner of town, our café quickly became a beloved local hotspot, attracting residents, workers, students, and visitors alike.
